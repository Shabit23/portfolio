03 : About me

Background

I have just received my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Engineering from Rajshahi University of Enginnering and Technology in Rajshahi which is a prestigious engineering university in Bangladesh. I am very passionate about programming and engineering, but above all I have a great deal of interest in learning something new. I primarily am a learner and very fond of complex systems that require critical thinking skills and logic. As such, I am very flexible and have worked in different fields like web development and android app development. I have also worked on face detection apps for my university project and also published a paper on Network Security this year!

Research Works

As I like to invest my time in learning new things, I found researching on a certain topic very interesting and exhilarating. To be honest, out of all my courses in university I found network security, machine learning and deep learning to be very exciting. To that end, I published a paper on Distributed Denial of Service or simply DDoS attack detection using Deep Learning techniques in IoT networks for which I had to study all my favorite courses. I would definitely like to research more on some other topics of network security including DDoS attacks and also possibly on AI in the future. The 'Works' tab in the website contains the details on my research paper.
